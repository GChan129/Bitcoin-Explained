What is blockchain?

Blockchain is the buzzword when it comes to cryptocurrency and Bitcoin. The conecpt of the blockchain is a bit tech heavy but what is really important to undersand is that the blockchain has solved a theoretical problem that had previously been unsolvable. The problem it solved is called the Byzantine Generals Problem. The solution to that solved the double spend problem and developed the concept of trustless trust.

The Double Spend Problem

The Double Spend Problem is when you have for example 1$ and you try to use that same dollar more than once. This doesn't happen in real life because when you purchase goods or services, you physically hand over your cash so you can't spend it again. On the internet, before the blockchain, there was no such thing as handing over an original. When I sent you a file, I sent you a copy of my file and then maybe deleated my file. When this comes to digital currency, if I or you have one internet dollar, who can stop us from copying that internet dollar to infinity? The blockchain solves this problem.



The Blockchain Explained

The most amazing thing about Bitcoin's blockchain is that Satoshi Nakamoto made it opensource! That's right. This revolutionary piece of technology is free for anyone to copy or use or improve. Just go to Github's Bitcoin page and you can look at Bitcoin's code. Out of this transparency a community, an industry and all of the other cryptocurrencies were born. That's why Bitcoin is truely a cryptocurrency for the people.
